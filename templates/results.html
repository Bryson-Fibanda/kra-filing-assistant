Common Mistakes to Avoid

📅
Wrong Year Selected

Filing in 2026 means declaring income earned in 2025. Double-check before submitting.

🔢
Wrong PAYE Figure

Enter the annual total PAYE from your P9 — not a monthly figure.

✅
Submitting Without Validating

Always click Validate before Submit. Validation catches errors before they become problems.

📥
Not Downloading the ZIP

iTax requires you to download the return ZIP and re-upload it before submitting.

🏢
Wrong Employer PIN

Enter your employer's KRA PIN exactly as it appears on your P9.

🧾
No Acknowledgement Receipt

After submitting, always download your acknowledgement receipt as proof of filing.

⏰

What If I Miss the June 30 Deadline?

Late Filing Penalty

KES 2,000 flat penalty for individuals who file after June 30, 2026.

Late Payment Penalty

5% of the tax due plus 1% interest per month if you owe tax and pay late.

Still File Even If Late

Filing late is always better than not filing. The penalty for never filing is much higher.

Apply for Waiver

If you have a genuine reason for missing the deadline, apply for a penalty waiver through iTax.
