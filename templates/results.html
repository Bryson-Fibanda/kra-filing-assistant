⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

📅
Wrong Year Selected

Filing in 2026 means declaring income earned in 2025. Double-check the year before submitting.

🔢
Wrong PAYE Figure

Enter the total annual PAYE deducted — not monthly. Check your P9 form for the annual total.

✅
Submitting Without Validating

Always click Validate before Submit on iTax. Validation catches errors before they become problems.

📥
Not Downloading the ZIP

iTax requires you to download the return ZIP file and re-upload it before submitting. Don't skip this step.

🏢
Wrong Employer PIN

Enter your employer's KRA PIN exactly as it appears on your P9 form. A wrong PIN will cause a mismatch.

🧾
No Acknowledgement Receipt

After submitting, always download your acknowledgement receipt from iTax as proof of filing.

⏰

What If I Miss the June 30 Deadline?

Late Filing Penalty

KES 2,000 flat penalty for individuals who file after June 30, 2026.

Late Payment Penalty

5% of the tax due plus 1% interest per month if you owe tax and pay late.

Still File Even If Late

Filing late is always better than not filing at all. The penalty for never filing is much higher.

Apply for Waiver

If you have a genuine reason for missing the deadline, you can apply for a penalty waiver through iTax.
